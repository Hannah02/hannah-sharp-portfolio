"Anglerfish"


"Anglerfish" is all about first impressions and gut feelings. On the surface of this digital painting, a beautiful hybrid woman is the first thing we see, along with a small adorable fish.
This could be interpreted as quite a calm scene but something primal immediately tells us there is more to this than what we immediately interpret and suddenly, the painting takes on a more eerie, sinister tone.


Created in Sketchbook and Photoshop.
