Film Poster-
"Perks of Being a Wallflower"

Inspired by the grid-layout of Josef Muller-Brockman, this is my design for a poster for one of my all time favourite films (and books), Stephen Chbosky's "The Perks of Being a Wallflower".

I created a geometric shape to be the main focus of the poster, which resembles a flower and is made up from many overlapped infinity signs, a reference to one of the most important lines of the book- "And in that moment, I swear we were infinite.".
