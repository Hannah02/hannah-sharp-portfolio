Akzidenz Grotesk-
Typeface Poster

In ths typeface poster, I wanted to provide as much information about the Akzidenz Grotesk typeface as I could while using visuals that I thought reflected the harsh angles of each letter. These angles definitely inspired the composition of this piece and, in my opinion, made it stand out visually from some other typeface posters.
