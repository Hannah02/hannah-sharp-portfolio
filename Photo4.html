Micro Photo No. 2

My second macro photo in the series and has taken a clear influence from one of my favourite art and film movements- German Expressionism. The harsh angles and edited colour of this image portray the buing in a new and unusual way.

The sharp corner and repetition of lines within the building, coupled with the yellow colour in the windows add a more creepy atmosphere to an otherwise average building and frames the architecture dramatically.
